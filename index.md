# Cedarwood Transportation

Cedarwood is a non-emergency medical transportation service. We provide wheelchair-accessible rides for the Lubbock area. 

Cedarwood is owned and operated by Winston and Angie Gentles. We've been serving the region since 2012, and we've carried clients safely through over 15,000 trips!

Call us at any time to schedule a pick up, or learn more about our services.
